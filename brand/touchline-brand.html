The Name

Why "Touchline"

  • It's where the manager stands.The touchline is the sideline a coach runs the match from — the exact vantage this app puts a club director in.
  • It's unmistakably soccer.A specific pitch term, not a generic "team" word — it stakes out the sport without spelling it out.
  • It carries "staying in touch."Parents, sponsors, treasurer — the double meaning fits a comms-and-money hub.
  • One word, easy to own.Short, no awkward spelling, works as an app name and a home-screen label.
